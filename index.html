Overview

Sri Mukkaneswara Associates was established in February 2021 with an initial membership of 8 members. However, one member voluntarily opted out due to personal reasons.

Despite this, the association has continued to move forward with determination and has successfully completed 4 years of operation. At the end of each year, the association conducts a meeting, and the expenses for this meeting are covered by the association's funds.

The members of the association are considered part of a single family, and each member contributes to the association's account. The association operates according to a set of rules, which can be found in the "Rules Section."

Key Information

  • Total Members: 7
  • Monthly Deposit: ₹1,500 per member
  • Total Monthly Collection: ₹10,500

Rules and Regulations

Membership Rules

  • All members must maintain active participation in the association
  • Members should attend all scheduled meetings without fail
  • Any absence must be informed in advance with valid reason

Financial Rules

  • Monthly deposits of ₹1,500 must be made before the 5th of each month
  • Late deposits may incur penalties as decided by the association
  • All financial transactions must be properly documented

Loan Rules

  • Loans are available only to active members
  • Interest rate is fixed at ₹1.50 per ₹100 per month
  • Loan repayment schedule must be strictly followed
  • Maximum loan amount depends on available funds

Meeting Rules

  • Monthly meetings are mandatory for all members
  • Annual meeting attendance is compulsory
  • Important decisions require majority consensus
  • Meeting minutes must be properly recorded

General Rules

  • Members must maintain transparency in all dealings
  • Any disputes will be resolved through association meetings
  • Rules can be amended with unanimous agreement
  • Members must maintain confidentiality of association matters
