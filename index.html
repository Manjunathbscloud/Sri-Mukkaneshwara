Overview

About Our Association

Sri Mukkanneshwara Associate was established in February 2021 with the vision of fostering financial cooperation and mutual growth among its members. Starting with 8 initial members, the association has evolved into a well-structured organization with 7 active members. The association maintains a systematic approach to financial management, with each member contributing ₹2,000 monthly, resulting in a total monthly collection of ₹14,000.

Our Journey

The association's journey began in 2021 with an initial monthly deposit of ₹1,000 per member. Our first annual meeting was conducted at M Thumbaraguddi (Native Place), where we established our core rules and loan procedures. In 2022, we strengthened our foundation by increasing the monthly deposit to ₹1,500 and held our second annual meeting at Sampige Heritage Resort, Koppal. During this phase, we implemented a streamlined loan distribution process and established systematic record-keeping practices.

The year 2023 marked a period of growth and stability. We successfully managed multiple active loans and strengthened member relationships through regular meetings. Our third annual meeting was held at Cotton County Club, Hubballi, where we reviewed and enhanced our financial management systems.

Current Status

Currently, in 2024-25, the association has further enhanced its financial structure. The monthly deposit has been increased to ₹2,000 per member, reflecting our growing strength and stability. We maintain transparent financial management with detailed record-keeping, and our loan management system continues to benefit all members effectively. The fourth annual meeting is planned to be held at Jungle Vibes Resort, Dandeli, where we will review our progress and plan future initiatives.

Key Achievements

Throughout our journey, we have achieved several significant milestones. We have established a robust financial framework, implemented transparent loan procedures, and maintained strong member relationships. Our systematic approach to record-keeping and financial management has ensured smooth operations and member satisfaction. The gradual increase in monthly deposits from ₹1,000 to ₹2,000 reflects our growing financial capacity and members' commitment to the association's growth.
