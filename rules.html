Rules and Regulations

Guidelines ensuring smooth operation of our association

Membership Rules

  • All members must attend monthly meetings
  • Monthly contribution of ₹2,000 is mandatory
  • Members must maintain active participation
  • New members require approval from existing members

Loan Rules

  • Maximum loan amount is ₹50,000 per member
  • Loan interest rate is 12% per annum
  • Minimum loan period is 6 months
  • Loan applications require 2 guarantors
  • Repayment must be made monthly

Administrative Guidelines

  • President serves for 2 years term
  • Monthly meetings on first Sunday
  • Annual meeting in December
  • All decisions require majority vote
  • Financial records must be maintained transparently
