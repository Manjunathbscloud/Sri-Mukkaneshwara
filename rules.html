Rules and Regulations

Membership Rules

  • Membership is valid for 5 years, members who want to exit this association within this period will not get any interest or benefit
  • Monthly deposit amount should be paid between 10th to 15th of the month
  • Every member should attend the annual meeting held in the month of December or November every year
  • Dates of annual meeting will be announced after discussion and finalization, every member must attend the meeting otherwise fine will be imposed
  • Member who wants to exit from Associate will get only the amount paid by him till date, he will not get any interest or share in profit
  • A person managing an associate is exempted from payment for one month

Financial Rules

  • Minimum balance of ₹5,000 should be maintained in associate account
  • Loans will be given only to fellow members
  • Interest rate is ₹1.5 per 100 per month
  • Interest must be paid on the loan taken along with the monthly fee
  • The loan taken must be repaid to the association within 1 year from the date of taking
  • The minimum loan amount available is ₹10,000
  • Maximum loan amount is ₹2.5 lakhs
  • Member should check associate balance before applying for loan, application will be rejected if requested loan amount is more than available balance
  • Borrowing member shall not be eligible for another loan irrespective of the consequences for 3 months

Administrative Guidelines

  • The decision taken by Manjunath and Pratap on behalf of the Associate in the near future shall be observed by the members and discussed with all the members before finalisation
