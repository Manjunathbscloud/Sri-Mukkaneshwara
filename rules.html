Rules and Regulations

Sri Mukkanneshwar Associates

M Tumbaraguddi

Membership Rules

  • Membership is valid for 5 years, no interest or benefits for early exit
  • Monthly deposit payment window: 10th to 15th of each month
  • Mandatory attendance at annual meetings
  • Late payment penalty of Rs. 100 per month
  • Members must maintain proper conduct during meetings
  • Membership can be transferred with committee approval

Financial Rules

  • Monthly deposit amount: Rs. 1000
  • Loan interest rate: 2% per month
  • Maximum loan amount: Up to total deposited amount
  • Emergency loans subject to committee approval

General Guidelines

  • Respect and maintain decorum during meetings
  • Changes to rules require majority committee approval
  • Grievances to be addressed to committee in writing
  • Annual audit reports will be shared with all members
