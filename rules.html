Rules and Regulations

Sri Mukkanneshwar Associates M Tumbaraguddi

Final Rules and Regulations

  1. Membership is valid for 5 years, members who want to exit this association within this period will not get any interest or benefit.
  2. Monthly deposit amount should be paid between 10th to 15th of the month.
  3. Loans will be given only to fellow members.
  4. Interest rate is ₹1.5 per 100 per month.
  5. Interest must be paid on the loan taken along with the monthly fee.
  6. The loan taken must be repaid to the association within 1 year from the date of taking.
  7. Borrowing member shall not be eligible for another loan irrespective of the consequences for 3 months.
  8. A person managing an associate is exempted from payment for one month.
  9. The minimum loan amount available is ₹10,000.
  10. Member should check associate balance before applying for loan, application will be rejected if requested loan amount is more than available balance.
  11. Maximum loan amount is ₹2.5 lakhs.
  12. Every member should attend the annual meeting held in the month of December or November every year.
  13. Dates of annual meeting will be announced after discussion and finalization, every member must attend the meeting otherwise fine will be imposed.
  14. Member who wants to exit from Associate will get only the amount paid by him till date, he will not get any interest or share in profit.
  15. Minimum balance of ₹5,000 should be maintained in associate account.
  16. The decision taken by Manjunath and Pratap on behalf of the Associate in the near future shall be observed by the members and discussed with all the members before finalisation.
